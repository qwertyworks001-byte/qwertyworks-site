Discord Rules

Please follow our community guidelines

Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or offensive content. Personal attacks are not allowed.

No sharing personal information (yours or others'). No NSFW, gore, or illegal content. Discord's Terms of Service must be followed at all times.

Post content in the correct channel. Avoid spamming or off-topic messages.

Do not advertise your games, Discord servers, or other content unless approved by staff.

Listen to moderators and admins. Arguing with staff may lead to a warning.

Only use bots in designated channels. Don't spam bot commands everywhere.

Do not ping or tag moderators/admins unnecessarily. Only tag staff if it's an urgent issue or emergency. Spamming staff pings may result in a warning.
